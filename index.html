About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at University of Houston. Previously, I was a Postdoc (CI-Fellow) working with my mentor Dr. Krishnendu Chakrabarty at Duke University. Before starting at Duke, I was a PhD candidate at Washington State University where I was advised by Dr. Partha Pratim Pande and Dr. Janardhan Rao Doppa. I completed my undergraduate at the Department of Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering, Jadavpur University, India. Apart from research, I enjoy going out on hikes and watching football (soccer). I also spend a lot of time reading about a variety of topics. You can find my CV here.

My research interests include:

  • Manycore systems
  • In-memory computing
  • Emerging technologies
  • Accelerating Deep Learning
  • Reliable and Secure Machine Learning
